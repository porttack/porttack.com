[NPR polled K-12 teachers about AI in their classrooms](https://www.npr.org/2026/06/05/nx-s1-5779757/school-ai-education-students-teachers-poll-critical-thinking), and the results don't sit easily next to each other:

<!--more-->

> More than half of teachers, 54%, say AI is making it harder for students to learn critical thinking skills.

And yet most of those same teachers expect AI to reshape education more than the internet or computers ever did. The people closest to the classroom think this technology is about to change everything, and they are not at all sure that's good news yet.
