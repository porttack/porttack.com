For 40 years, people have paid me to play with computers. I've solved problems for a living. I started early in high school finding interesting things to discover in electronics and computer science. Always learning.

I've tried a dozen times to pass on my passions for electronics and computers to my own children and their classmates -- sometime successfully -- always worth the effort.

It is time to eliminate other distractions.
