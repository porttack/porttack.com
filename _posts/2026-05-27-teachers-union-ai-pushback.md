[The American Federation of Teachers, 1.8 million members strong, wants schools to slow down on AI](https://www.edweek.org/technology/teachers-unions-ai-plan-calls-for-big-tech-tax-screen-bans-in-elementary-schools/2026/05):

<!--more-->

> The union wants to ban all screen use under grade 3, all "student-facing" artificial intelligence in elementary school, and "companion chatbots" for students under age 16.

Worth reading in full. Whatever you think of the specific asks, a union this size saying "let's slow down and think" is a sign the conversation in education is shifting, from how fast we can adopt AI to whether we should, and for whom.
